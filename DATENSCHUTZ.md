Privacy Policy

This app stores the data you enter in the app in a Supabase database so that the app can provide its core functionality.

I do not sell your data.
I do not use your data for advertising.
I do not use your data for tracking.
I do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

Data stored by the app may include your account information, such as your email address if you create an account, and the content you create or save inside the app.

The app uses Supabase as a backend service provider. Data is stored on Supabase servers and may be accessible to the app developer for support, debugging, maintenance, and security purposes.
